INSTRUMENT OF DESIGNATION AND DELEGATION 
I, Lena Metlege Diab, Minister of Citizenship and Immigration, repeal the former Immigration Instrument of Delegation dated March 11, 2025 and, 
pursuant to section 6 of the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act (the Act), hereby establish: 
Persons whose position is set out in Column 4 of an item of the attached schedule (designated officers) to carry out the powers and duties set out in 
column 3 of that item for the purpose of the provision(s) of the Act and/or Immigration and Refugee Protection Regulations (the Regulations) set out in 
column 2 of that item; 
Persons designated as officers to carry out the powers and duties set out in column 3 of the item for the purpose of the provision(s) of the Act and/or 
Regulations set out in column 2 of that item include 

an official of the Department of Citizenship and Immigration (CIC)1 or of the Canada Border Services Agency (CBSA) who is required to 
temporarily replace an officer whose position is set out in column 4 of that item or who is required by his supervisor to temporarily carry out the 
duties of that position; 

a CIC official that has direct or indirect supervisory responsibility for an officer whose position is set out in column 4 of that item; and, 

a CBSA officer that has direct or indirect supervisory responsibility for an officer whose position is set out in column 4 of that item; 
A person whose position is set out in column 4 of an item of the attached schedule is authorized to do anything that may be done by me and that is 
described in column 3 of that item for the purpose of the provision(s) of the Act and/or Regulations set out in column 2 of that item; 

Persons authorized to do anything that may be done by me and that is described in column 3 of an item for the purpose of the provision(s) of the 
Act and/or Regulations set out in column 2 of that item include 
o
a CIC or CBSA official who is required to temporarily replace a person whose position is set out in column 4 of that item or who is 
required by his supervisor to temporarily carry out the duties of that position; 
o
a CIC official that has direct or indirect supervisory responsibility for a person whose position is set out in column 4 of that item; and 
o
a CBSA officer that has direct or indirect supervisory responsibility for a person whose position is set out in column 4 of that item.
